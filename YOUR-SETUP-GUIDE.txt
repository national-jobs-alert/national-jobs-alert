================================================================================
  TROUBLESHOOTING
================================================================================

Problem: Sidebar says "Sheet not reachable — showing built-in jobs"
  → Check Step 2 (Share → Anyone with link → Viewer)
  → Use START-WEBSITE.bat, not double-click index.html

Problem: Some jobs missing
  → Row must have a title in column B
  → Do not leave title empty

Problem: Job shows wrong info
  → Check you did not skip columns (each column must align with header)

Problem: Website still shows old jobs after sheet change
  → Press F5 (hard refresh: Ctrl+F5)
  → Make sure you edited the correct sheet tab (first tab, gid=0)
