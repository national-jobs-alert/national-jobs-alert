💡 Pro Tips to Increase Typing Speed

1
Maintain Home Row discipline

Keep fingers resting lightly on ASDF (left hand) and JKL; (right hand) keys. Return home blindly.

2
Do not look down at keyboard

Keep your eyes fixed on the screen. Let your fingers build strong muscle memory naturally over time.

3
Focus on Accuracy first

Speed is a direct byproduct of accuracy. Slow down to stay above 95% accuracy, then push your speed limits.

4
Look 1-2 words ahead

Read ahead of the word you are typing. This helps your fingers plan motions and execute them fluidly without pause.

5
Phonetic Urdu Layout

For Urdu, standard phonetic keyboards map similar sounds (e.g. 'A' for Alif, 'B' for Bay). Practice this layout for local tests.

6
Govt Exam Standards

Junior Clerks require 30 WPM minimum. Data Entry Operators require 40 WPM. Stenographers need 50+ WPM speed.
